Austar (Paclobutrazole 23%SC)

Coupona (Paclobutrazole 40% SC)

Coupona (Paclobutrazole 40% SC) is a High-Concentration Systemic Plant Growth Regulator that inhibits vegetative growth to promote earlier, abundant flowering and increased fruit set, particularly in crops like mango.

Ethevin (Ethephon 39%SL)

Ethevin (Ethephon 39% SL) is a Systemic Plant Growth Regulator that releases ethylene within the plant tissue, promoting various processes like fruit ripening, flower induction, and uniform leaf drop.
